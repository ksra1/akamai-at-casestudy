Sr. Technology Project Manager(TPM) Panel Interview — Candidate Scenario
Your total interview time will be 90 minutes. You will have 45 minutes for your presentation,
including time for questions during your talk. After your presentation there will be an additional
30 min for the panel to ask further questions, and then 15 minutes where you are welcome to
ask us any questions.. You may use PowerPoint or similar tools to support your presentation.
Scenario Overview
You have recently been assigned as the Project Manager for AT Retailers, a global e-commerce
organization undergoing a major digital transformation. AT Retailers operates worldwide, with
high traffic originating from North America, LATAM, and Europe. The company is moving from a
legacy monolithic architecture to a modern microservices and API-driven landscape hosted
across on-prem infrastructure and AWS.
Over the past several months, AT Retailers has acquired several companies and they need to
migrate them from their existing cloud provider over to Akamai.
Additionally their existing sites are seeing security issues with bot attacks that use credential
stuffing and scraping and the team needs your help addressing those issues.
In your role as the Project Manager, you are responsible for evaluating the problems, designing
a high-level architecture, and outlining the delivery and security plan.
Key Challenges Identified by AT Retailers
1. Delivery Issues
Customer has given us 30 days to get 5,000 hostnames onboarded
New customer teams aren't trained in Akamai products yet will support the new
onboardings.
Sites need to be able to handle 5x traffic during peak events
Existing images are sometimes slow to load
Customer indicates testing is not necessary, they prefer to push to production then fix
issues.
e Handling of customer resources across multiple geographies(Europe, US, Asia) as well
as managing internal Akamai resources.
2. Bot Activity and Automated Threats
DDOS attacks attempting to take down customers origin
Legacy WAF Rule Sets out of date on some configurations
Surge in credential stuffing attempts
Inventory scraping by competitive bots
Carding attempts targeting checkout APIs
Bots using sophisticated evasion techniques (IP rotation, spoofed clients)
Your Task for the Interview Presentation
You will prepare a 45-minute presentation addressing the following:
A) Delivery & Project Management Strategy
Outline how you would lead the end-to-end delivery of this program, including:
Phases of implementation (assessment, rollout, hardening, optimization)
Coordination with multiple teams
Change management
Risk management and escalation approach
B) High-Level Security Architecture Using Akamai Products
Design a solution using Akamai’s delivery and security portfolio. Your proposed architecture
should show how we can onboard the customers’ hostnames, solve their traffic and load
problems as well as address the WAF, Bot, and API security challenges for both new and old
sites.
C) Security Problem Analysis
Provide your assessment of the situation, including:
e Key risks and root causes
e Impact on the business (operational, financial, security posture)
e How WAF, Bot, and API security issues interrelate in this environment
Deliverables for the Interview
A structured 30-minute presentation with slides
High-level solution architecture using Akamai Products
Clear Project Management style roadmap and execution plan
Mention the competitive advantages of Akamai over other vendors in your proposed
solution.
e Mention one Akamai Best Practice per product in your solution. (Akamai Internal
candidates only)
